How does this work?

Once you provide us the basic words that describe your business our service uses highly advanced computer algorithms to generate relevant keywords. Eventually, the best selection of words are chosen and showed to the user. Injecting those keywords into your site will increase your traffic.


What are the TOS(Terms of Service)?

This service is a free POC(Proof of Concept). Therefore only basic keywords are returned and they are not being analyzed for context. If you're interested in the idea and want to test the full features/service please contact me.
